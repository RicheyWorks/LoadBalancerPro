Decision Explorer

A read-only, simulation-only reviewer surface for exploring DecisionExplorerPayloadV1 data returned by POST /api/routing/decision-explorer. It uses caller-provided synthetic routing telemetry and already-built compare evidence only.

Safety Boundary
